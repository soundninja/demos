i

Carneyval has teamed up with INSTRUM for this remix of Kendrick Lamar's track "i". The vocal track used for this remix was originally a cover version performed by Jackson Breit, whom Carneyval has worked with on several tracks. The result is a clubby and upbeat track, with some catchy chords to hum along to. There's a free download you can pick up too. Enjoy :)
