Danielle Bradbery Is Squad Goals in New “Friend Zone” Video (Watch!)

Jen Swirsky October 14, 2015 Danielle Bradbery, Music Video, New Music

Danielle Bradbery released her “Friend Zone” music video to CMT overnight, showing that being “friend zoned” by Danielle is actually a great place to be. Featuring a slew of the Big Machine artist’s beautiful squad, the video about making one of your girls realize that the guy she wants is nothing more than platonic softens the blow because she is supported by friends.

The video shows Danielle and her girls having fun, living freely, and enjoying time together without the unnecessary stress of questioning a relationship status. Whether the girls are dancing outdoors, skateboarding around town, or simply enjoying one another’s company, Danielle and her team prove that living in the friend zone when you are otherwise surrounded by love isn’t so bad after all.

Watch Danielle Bradbery’s “Friend Zone” video now, exclusively on CMT.com!
