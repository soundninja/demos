Electric Daisy Carnival Orlando Drops Biggest Lineup Yet

EDC Orlando’s massive lineup has been announced! The exciting fifth anniversary of Electric Daisy Carnival Orlando is scheduled to awe crowds at Tinker Field on November 6th and 7th. Over 50 artists are planned to perform on EDC’s three magnificant stages over the weekend.

“I’m so happy to return to Tinker Field for our fifth EDC in Orlando,” said Pasquale Rotella, the Founder and CEO of Insomniac. “Dance music has strong roots in Florida, and our Headliners there bring so much creativity and positive energy. We can’t wait to bring them a great show.”

EDC Orlando promises to showcase not only some of the hardest working producers, but a massive playground full of carnival rides, performers, and some amazing art installations. Partygoers can enjoy the kineticFIELD, circuitGROUNDS, and neonGARDEN as well as The Dandelion Forest and much more. The Dandelion Forest will bloom inside the festival grounds along with the return of the peaceful Buddha art installation. The festival boasts seven gigantic carnival attractions.

Two-day general admission tickets for EDC Orlando are on sale now for $169 before service fees. VIP tickets are also available for guests 21 and older for $279 before fees. VIP amenities include a separate entrance to the festival for expedited access, exclusive decks with a full cash bar overlooking the stage, private restroom facilities and a souvenir laminate and lanyard. Payment plans are available for both ticket options. EDC Orlando is an 18 and older event.

Tickets are available at Orlando.ElectricDaisyCarnival.com.
