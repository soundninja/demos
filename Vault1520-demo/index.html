Drake and Willow Smith Collaboration May be in the Works

Drake’s highly anticipated project, Views from the Six, is slated for release this summer, and fans are more than excited to hear it. The Canadian rapper shocked us all when he pulled a Beyonce, and dropped his mixtape, If You’re Reading this it’s Too Late, back in February. Since then, he’s been featured on numerous tracks in usual fashion, showing that crazy work ethic that he’s famous for.

This weekend, Drake hinted that Willow Smith may be featured on his upcoming album. Both artists were performing at Wireless Festival in London, and he posted a photo of the young singer on his Instagram page with the caption “Young muse all on views.” Obviously it’s open to interpretation, but we hope that our suspicions are true.
