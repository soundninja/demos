Hailing from the sunny Los Angeles, Oyster Kids sure know how to keep summer last just a liiiittle longer, which we thank them for. Debut single Creepy ticks all the sweet indie pop boxes (think St. Lucia x Sir Sly x Pacific Air x all of your fave cute male pop stars), yet there’s a darker side boiling underneath the (really) pretty surface. We kind of wanna throw a depressed party with sad smiley face balloons right now, and put Creepy on repeat for the entire evening. And it would absolutely perfect.
