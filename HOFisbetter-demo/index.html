Erykah Badu – Hotline Bling

Joerelle Rivera
October 2, 2015
#BETTERGIRLS, Featured, Music

It’s been a minute since Erykah Badu has blessed our ears with new sounds, but yesterday she took to Facebook to announce the release of a new mixtape “BUT YOU CAINT USE MY PHONE”.

JAH BLESS. I’VE NEEDED NEW ERYKAH IN MY LIFE.

And with that news, she dropped her own version of this Summer’s “Marvin’s Room 2.0” hit, Hotline Bling.

There has been many a cover of the Drake classic (i.e Disclosure and Sam Smith, Kehlani and Charlie Puth, etc), but none have come close to the standard Ms. Badu has set with this remix.

Although I’m not a huge fan of the first quarter of the song with Badu auto-tuned, the magic happens at the 4 minute mark when we hear the familiar gypsy bells (from The Healer – can we YouTube link to her song?) and that celestial sound of hers we know and love.

Don’t sleep and listen to Erykah Badu’s take on “Hotline Bling” below.
