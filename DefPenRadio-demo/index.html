Ty Dolla $ign – ‘LA’ (Feat. Kendrick Lamar, Brandy, & James Fauntleroy)

Travis G. @yoyotrav

Ty Dolla $ign – ‘LA’ (Feat. Kendrick Lamar, Brandy, & James Fauntleroy)

02000

In a little over a week, Ty Dolla $ign will unleash his debut album, Free TC, to the world. Over the past year, the singer/rapper has crafted gems for not only himself, but for plenty of the who’s who of today’s booming music industry. Achieving Urban hits left to right, Ty Dolla $ign is looking to put the focus on himself and drop off his long-awaited LP. Most recently premiering his Wiz Khalifa assisted record, ‘Sittin’ Pretty,’ along with the guitar-driven record, ‘Soild,’ with Babyface, Ty has another new treat for us with ‘LA.’

If the record’s title wasn’t enough, ‘LA’ drips in West coast swagger in ever facet imaginable. To embody everything about the city, Ty strategically called on Kendrick Lamar, Brandy, and James Fauntleroy, who all have found their humble beginnings started in California. Spitting a flurry of rhymes and highlighting key staples of the city, Kendrick Lamar reps hard for his city while Brandy and James Fauntleroy hold things down with their harmonious vocals and singing of their admiration of the city. Serving as the perfect opener for Free TC, the city of Los Angeles has a record they can be proud off. Check out the record, below, and be sure to pick up Ty Dolla $ign’s debut album on November 13th.
