FCX: 10 YEARS OF FUTURE CLASSIC

A huge thank you to everyone who helped us celebrate our FCX: 10 Years of Future Classic birthday event. In collaboration with Vivid LIVE, we were fortunate enough to party on the Sydney Opera House’s outdoor broadwalk with sets from Flume, Flight Facilities, Seekae, Wave Racer, Hayden James, Touch Sensitive & Charles Murdoch.

Check out the full run of pics here!

Big love to everyone who has championed our artists & danced at our parties over the last TEN years. Thank you for listening! xxx
