Kanye West and Paul McCartney have been showing that non-rap music legends and current rappers can make great music together since the beginning of the year when "Only One" was released. Then A$AP Rocky came along and claimed that he was making a mixtape with Rod Stewart called Born To Be Pretty. It sounded like a pretty awesome idea, and the title was incredible, but it all turned out to be a joke.

Rocky got us thinking, though, what other legendary musicians would pair well with current rappers, and what would their debut mixtape or album be called? Read on to meet Tom Petty & The Heartdrakers, Keef Richards and, of course, Future Hendrix.
