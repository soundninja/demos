Showing Their Softer Side

7/2/2015

 
The Chainsmokers are fully satisfied with this song and they have a lot to be proud of. They co-wrote the lyrics, Drew sings on the hook, and they made it themselves. Ladywood has been witness to the movements of this duo since before they were Selfie susans and I've got to say this is my favorite smokie track; it's silky smooth. -R
