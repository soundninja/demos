[Knock of The Day] – “Can’t Feel My Face” – The Weeknd (Keljet Remix)

25 Jun 2015
Staff
Off

Netherlands based indie-disco production duo Keljet have been tearing up dance floors and inciting parties for over a half decade. With support from industry heavyweights like Kygo, The Knocks, Oliver Heldens, Goldroom and Zimmer, the homie’s pop meets tropical disco sound can make anyone get up and dance… even your grandma. Here they are with their newest material, a funky bootleg of The Weeknd’s “Can’t Feel My Face.”
