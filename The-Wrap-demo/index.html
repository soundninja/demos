And the winner is …

NBC’s “The Voice” crowned a champion on Tuesday night, when Sawyer Fredericks from Team Pharrell took the whole darn thing.

Behind the big winner, Megan Lindsey was runner-up, Joshua Davis came in third and Koryn Hawthorne finished fourth. As part of the prize package, Sawyer is set to appear on the “Today” show Thursday.

Also on Tuesday, NBC released a pretty funny video of “Voice” judge Christina Aguilera doing fairly spot-on impressions of Britney Spears, Shakira, Lady Gaga, Cher, Sia and Miley Cyrus. The “Genie in a Bottle” singer took a page out of “Saturday Night Live’s” impersonations manual, using faux auditions to house her talents for mimicry.

Readers can watch that video above.
