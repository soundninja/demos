5SOS to visit HMV to celebrate the release of their new album ‘Sounds Good Feels Good’

Oct - 29 2015 | no comments

Written by: Staff Writer on October 29, 2015.

5SOS visit hmv Glasgow Argyle Street to meet fans and sign copies of their new album ‘Sounds Good Feels Good’. 300 lucky fans secured their place at the event by pre-ordering the album.

5 Seconds of Summer, the biggest breakthrough band of 2014, release their eagerly anticipated new album ‘Sounds Good Feels Good’. The record follows their self-titled debut album, which sold in excess of three million copies worldwide making it the biggest US debut album of the year.

‘Sounds Good Feels Good’ sees the band collaborate again with rock veteran John Feldmann (producer All Time Low, Good Charlotte, Boys Like Girls) who has executively produced the album. The result is an album packed with adrenaline-fuelled pop punk hits that give a nod to their super-rock turn-of-the-millennium US Alt-Rock influences. The band spent three months living together in a studio in LA writing and recording an album that they have poured their hearts and souls in to and are very proud of. Ashton says, “We put our heart and soul into Sounds Good Feels Good. I’ve never been more proud of anything in my life… It’s for you!”

Thursday 5th November – 4:00pm – hmv Glasgow Argyle Street (Sold Out)
