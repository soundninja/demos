Avenue: Our Love (Feat. Tokyo Police Club)

August 11, 2015  /  Darren Bonilla

Over the last year Toronto’s Avenue, aka Sam Willows, has put his classical training to work on high-profile remixes for Miami Horror, Citizens!, and Penguin Prison. Now the twenty-one year old wunderkind of flashy funk has teamed with Tokyo Police Club to bring the first bit of original work dubbed “Our Love.”

Greeted by synths-galore, Willows wastes little time bringing the heavy disco-beat we all need to get moving across the dance floor. The bass line digs deep before a delicate breakdown strips the tune down to a grand piano, vocal duet and we’re assured that “...there’s no end to our love.”

The EP will be release October 2nd via Haven Sounds and will be supported by a U.S. tour, so keep your ear to the ground.
