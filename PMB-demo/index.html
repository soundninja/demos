Krept & Konan Take Straight Outta Compton All the Way to South London!

27 Aug, 2015 0 Let Me Tell You About... / Music

With Straight Outta Compton set for release in UK cinemas tomorrow, rap duo Krept and Konan drew inspiration from the film and took it upon themselves to demonstrate this visually.

They recently had Ice Cube's son O’Shea Jackson Jr. and Jason Mitchell (who plays Eazy E in the NWA biopic) visit their local areas Gipsy Hill and Thornton Heath, both in South London. They were able to gain an insight into Krept and Konan's background as well as exchanging stories and realising the similarities in their life experiences, despite living in different parts of the world.

The Straight Outta London visual acts as an inspirational message to those who perhaps aren’t completely content or satisfied with their lifestyle, and that it’s important to aim high and chase your dreams!
